How We Work!

How We Work

  • Step 1
    Fill out our mentorship form linked below.
  • Step 2
    We will contact you through Whatsapp within a few days of the form being filled.
  • Step 3
    Once you respond to our Whatsapp messages, appropriate mentors are assigned to the mentees.
  • Step 4
    We create Whatsapp groups for the mentor and the mentees. All guidance/mentorship will happen within the groups where all your queries will be cleared.
  • Step 5
    We will inform you of any updates, resources, webinars going to be conducted through Whatsapp itself.
