About

Welcome to Catalyst! An Educational and networking platform to help you connect, interact and draw from the experiences of the success stories of alumni of Indian schools in Oman! We wish to offer you the best takeaway from any experiences shared- whatever be your stream or your passion. We at Catalyst are aiming to provide the perfect mentoring, since at every point in life when it comes to making major career choices, we seek for people to guide us. We are aiming to support the students in overcoming systematic barriers on their journey from school to a college degree.
