Stepping Aside From The Usual

17/6/20212 min readRiya Reji

I graduated highschool from Indian School Al Seeb in commerce stream knowing very well what I wanted to pursue in my next level of studies. It was not something everyone would be happy about(or at least some of my relatives weren't). Keeping all the negatives aside, I've always wanted to be the first teacher in my family and I knew I could do it only if I stepped up for myself.

Growing up I always admired teachers and their dedication towards their field of work and as I have always loved reading books, watching movies and doing art. I was automatically inclined to pursue English as my Bachelor's Degree. There was a tough competition in getting into degree colleges than I ever expected and when I got admitted at Madras Christian College(MCC), I felt like I hit the jackpot.

Proper guidance can not only help a student be motivated for the goal but it also builds interests in the base knowledge of their stream which is very important.

MCC not only brought so many opportunities and encouragement to showcase my talents, but also made learning super fun. TV shows, music and movies are incorporated into our syllabus which makes it interesting all the more. (For instance we've had Harry Potter and Game of thrones references in one of our semester). Not only are they updated, but they are also open to all new ideas from our teenage minds. They are a very open group of professors I have ever come across and they are just like our best friends.

Being among one of the oldest group of colleges in India, dating back to 1837,yet MCC still stands beautiful bringing in so much joy to people who come visit the campus. The aura and vibe that MCC brings is one of the main things I miss while attending online classes.

I have personally seen English as a degree being belittled by people whereas it is so much more than a language. It talks about so many cultures and moral values and not just poets and authors. There's a whole world you can paint with your imagination through this subject and the brush is all yours. Even if it's being a writer, an editor or a professor, the only thing that matters is whether you're enjoying what you do and you give your hundred percent at your goals. So step out from the ordinary, get your goals straight and start hustling. The world is a seashell and it is all yours.
