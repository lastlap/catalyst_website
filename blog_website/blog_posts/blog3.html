Hitting the Bulls-eye: Pursuing ACCA with St.Joseph's

  • 12/6/2021
  • Tony Benny

Diving into the commerce world has definitely provided me with this new found interest in business. If you’re an aspiring commerce student, then you’d know that accounts and economics are not just mere subjects. They convey a story on how a business thrives in the real world.

After graduating from 10th grade, I knew for a fact that commerce would equip me with the relevant knowledge and skills that we need to take on the real world. Accountancy is a subject that I always like to practice. I’m not denying that it is challenging but that’s exactly what excited me! My liking towards the subject slowly grew into a passion and by the time I graduated 12th , I decided that B.Com with ACCA was just right for me.

ACCA will continue to grow for the years to come and I cannot wait to be a part of that journey. Businesses operate in a very dynamic environment and will always require qualified chartered accountants to support them. If you’re hard-working, interested in the subject and have a knack for numbers then ACCA will surely suit you. However, make sure to be careful. Parents and society may push you to take it up, but not everything in commerce has to be about CA! Just follow what your heart says.

Researching and applying to colleges can seem quite overwhelming, especially when you’ve got a huge a list of great colleges. I always developed a liking towards St.Joseph’s College of Commerce because of it’s excellence in teaching and it’s welcoming and family-like culture. But to get in was definitely a task!

Throughout my high school years, I had to make sure I scored consistently well and perform in extra-curriculars as much as I could. The overall experience was amazing! To participate with my fellow teammates, win competitions and make new friends will be something that I will always cherish.

Taking care of your academics as well as extra-curricular work should always be seen as a joyride and NOT be done for the sake of it.

I was glad that my college looked beyond my grades and tried to understand me as a person. This was done through a personal interview.

And what better way to receive your confirmation letter on your birthday.

Earning my seat in one of the most prestigious commerce college in India will definitely be an experience I will hold close to my heart.

While the whole online mode has definitely been a bummer, the college made sure to engage the students and keep them active by holding various online business fests, competitions and events. The work ethic is just extraordinary.

To all those who’re finding a way to get into their dream college, I strongly advice to keep working for it. With the right motivation and hard-work, the admission letter will be in your hands in no time.
