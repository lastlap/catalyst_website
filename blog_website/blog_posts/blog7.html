What is BMS and what are its Career options?

  • 26/6/2021
  • Maitri Asher

Bachelors in Management Studies, commonly known as BMS is an undergraduate program for management studies offered by many universities throughout the world. The course allows you to obtain the knowledge and skills needed to assume management positions in a wide range of organizations. Management Studies allows students to pick their electives between Finance, Management and Human Resources as per their area of interest.

Career Options

There is a huge demand for professionals from a management background in the corporate sector. With the increasing demand and tremendous opportunities, graduates of the BMS course have an extra edge over students from other backgrounds. Below are a few career options that can be taken up post BMS undergrad degree:

  1. Financial Consulting
  2. Administration and Operations
  3. Project Management (Executive Level)
  4. HR Management and Development
  5. Data Analytics
  6. Research Analyst
  7. Financial Management
  8. Communications Management
  9. Sales and Marketing

BMS from Narsee Monjee College of Commerce and Economics (NMCCE)

NMCCE is one of the top commerce colleges in the country and is affiliated to the University of Mumbai. Graduating with a BMS degree from NMCCE has various perks:

  1. NMCCE has one of the best faculties in Mumbai
  2. It is the leading college for BMS course
  3. It has an almost 100% placement record
  4. Country’s top companies pick students for placements

On a personal note, being a BMS graduate from NMCCE, I have seen the exposure and the importance of graduating from a top college in the country, be it an under-graduation or a post-graduation degree. It always gives you an edge over others.

College admission might be a tedious process but make sure to work hard to get into your dream college and success will follow you.
