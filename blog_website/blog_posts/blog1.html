MD - A great alternative for MBBS

  • 11/6/2021
  • Ruba Balu

Confused? Scared? Perplexed? Have no idea what to do further?

Well, you are in the right place cause we were in the same boots as yours. Pursuing an MD course can be a dream... But it doesn't come to reality for everyone. We always wish for the path that we choose to be the best one for ourselves. But deploying the right path means knowing how to tackle the oncoming problems.

I, just like you, had no idea how my path was going to be written. After NEET, there are two possible outcomes for our future pillars: come out with flying colours or get into a breakdown as of not knowing what to do further. And believe me, nothing can be more stressful than thinking about what to do further with your life. You might think about choosing a completely different career, but is it really worth it? How are you going to pursue your career with so many thoughts pondering in your mind?

I, myself, was trapped in this situation few years back. The fact that I couldn't clear NEET to get a good college in India bugged me. I felt like the world was on my shoulders...so much hardwork and efforts put into and the end result didn't seem fruitful enough. But there was 1 thing I didn't lose - Hope. I tried to research upon various career opportunities which had a good scope in future but my mind raced back to medical...I knew I was born for it...that was my destiny...it was then I came upon MD course.

Upon further research I realized that this course in the Caribbean was just the right thing for me. This course rolled only about 4 years whereas other courses like MBBS took about 5-6 years for completion. Studying MD in the Caribbean is highly valued and recognized which can easily land us some great job opportunities. Another huge plus is that we study in English here unlike countries like Russia or Germany where we need to pursue the courses in the native languages. For landing medical jobs in the US, we have to clear USMLE, for which we get in-class coaching in the Caribbean's MD course. We also get the splendid opportunity to do residency programme in the US. And that's what sold me! Within the next few months, I saw myself landing in AAIMS, Jamaica, one of the best recognized Caribbean Colleges for MD pursuers.
